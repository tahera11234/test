GET TO KNOW YOUR LEVEL

Knowing your level helps you train smarter, it ensures you focus on skills that match your current strength and ability, reducing the risk of injury and frustration. By choosing the right level, youll get accurate guidance, build proper foundations, and progress faster toward more advanced skills.

Start where you truly are, thats how real progress happen

.

🌱 Beginner

If youre new to bodyweight training or still building basic strength and control.

At this stage, the focus is on mastering fundamental movements like push-ups, pull-ups (or assisted), squats, and planks, while improving balance, coordination, and endurance.

With enough practice, you can still unlock cool skills aswell!🔥

⚡ Intermediate

An intermediate in calisthenics has built a solid foundation of strength and control. They can perform core movements consistently and begin progressing to more challenging variations, focusing on improving technique, endurance, and learning new skills that require lots of strength 💪

intermediate →

🚀 Advanced

Has a high level of strength, control, and body awareness. They can perform complex movements and static holds such as weighted muscle-ups, handstands, and levers, with strong techniques. This stage you can learn very complex skills that you can rarely find people perform 💯
