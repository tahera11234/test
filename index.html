What is gravity?

A phrase said by pro athletes, why do they say that? since the skills we learn can make us control our entire body with ease.

Calisthenics is a sports discipline based on bodyweight training. We can define it as the art of training with your own body: your body becomes the ultimate gym equipment you’ll use for your workouts, enabling physical improvement. With calisthenics, you’ll learn to use your body to enhance strength, hypertrophy, and coordination, resulting in a stronger body.
